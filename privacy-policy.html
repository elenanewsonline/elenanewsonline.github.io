3. Data Protection

  • Secure Storage: Your data is securely stored and accessed only by me or authorized personnel.

4. Your Rights

  • Access: You may request access to any personal data I hold about you at any time.

I retain personal records only as long as necessary to meet legal and operational requirements. After that, data is securely deleted.

My website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.


7. Changes to This Policy
